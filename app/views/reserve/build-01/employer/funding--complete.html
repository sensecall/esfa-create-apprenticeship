Making changes

You can add or change the apprenticeship training when you add an apprentice’s details.

You cannot change:

  • the off-the-job training start date, outside of the 3 month window you selected

To select a different apprenticeship start date, you will need to start again and secure new funding.

What you need to do now

If you know who your apprentice and training provider will be, you can add the apprentice’s details.

If you do not know who the apprentice or training provider will be, you will need to:

  • Find an apprentice. You can use our recruitment service to find an apprentice
  • Find a training provider. Use the find apprenticeship training service to find a training provider who offers the course you would like.
