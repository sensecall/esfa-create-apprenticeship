Secure funding for taking on an apprentice

{{releaseVersion | log }}

Use this service to secure government funding for a non-levy employer to train an apprentice. Government funding will pay for part or all of the cost of training an apprentice.

Before you start you will need:

  • permission from a non-levy employer that you can secure funds for them
  • an estimate of which month the apprenticeship training course will start
  • an estimate of the apprenticeship training course the apprentice may take, which can be changed later (some apprenticeship training courses have funding restrictions)
You can only secure funding up to 6 months before apprenticeship training is about to start.
