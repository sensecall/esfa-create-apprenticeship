Government funding will pay for part, or all of the cost of training an apprentice. Go to apprenticeship funding to find out how much you are allowed.

Before you start, you will need:

  • the month the apprenticeship training will start
  • which apprenticeship training course the apprentice will take (optional)
You can only secure funding up to 6 months before an apprenticeship training is about to start.
