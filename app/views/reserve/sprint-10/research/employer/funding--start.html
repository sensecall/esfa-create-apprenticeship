Check and reserve funding for taking on an apprentice

Government funding can pay for part or all of the cost of training an apprentice - up to the funding band maximum. You may need to pay up to a maximum of 5% of the total cost of the training.

To ensure government funds are available for your apprenticeship training, you will need reserve funding.

The reservation can be no more than 6 months in advance of the apprenticeâ€™s planned apprenticeship training start date.

What you'll need

To reserve government funding you will need:

  • an estimate of which month the apprentice will start their apprenticeship training, which can be flexible up to 3 months
  • the apprenticeship training the apprentice will take, which can be changed later
