You can print this confirmation screen to show your training provider that you have secured funding.

Making changes

You can change which apprenticeship training the apprentice will take, when you add an apprentice’s details. To make any other changes, you will need to start again and secure new funding.

What you need to do now

If you know who your apprentice and training provider will be, you can add the apprentice’s information.

If you do not know who the apprentice or training provider will be, follow these steps:

  1. Find an apprentice. You can use our recruitment service to find an apprentice
  2. Find a training provider. Use the find apprenticeship training service to find a training provider who offers the course you would like.
  3. Add information about your apprentice
