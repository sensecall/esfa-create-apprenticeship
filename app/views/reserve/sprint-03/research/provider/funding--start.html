Secure funding for non-levy employers

{{releaseVersion | log }}

Use this service to secure government funding for a non-levy employer to train an apprentice.

Before you start you will need:

  • permission from a non-levy employer to secure funds for them
  • which month the apprenticeship training will start
  • an estimate of which apprenticeship training the apprentice will take (optional)
You can only secure funding up to 6 months before the apprenticeship training is about to start.
