Get funding for non-levy employers

{{releaseVersion | log }}

Use this service to secure government funding for a non-levy employer to train an apprentice. Government funding will pay for part or all of the cost of training an apprentice.

Before you start you will need:

  • permission from a non-levy employer that you can secure funds for them
  • an estimate of which month the apprenticeship training course will start, which can be flexible up to 3 months
  • an idea of the apprenticeship training the apprentice will take, which can be changed later
