Secure funding for taking on an apprentice

{{releaseVersion | log }}

Use this service to secure government funding for training an apprentice. Government funding will pay for 95% of the cost of training an apprentice. You need to pay 5%.

{{ govukWarningText({ html: "From April 2019, you will not be able to secure any funding for training an aprentice. Any funding you have secured before April 2019 will not be affected.", iconFallbackText: "Current Funding restrictions" }) }}

Before you start you will need:

  • an estimate of which month the apprentice will start
  • an estimate of what training course the apprentice may take, which can be changed later

You can only secure funding up to 6 months before an apprenticeship is about to start.
