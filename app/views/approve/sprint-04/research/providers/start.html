Add and update apprentice records as a training provider

This service is for training providers registered with the apprenticeship service to work with employers to:

  • add details of the apprentices they're going to train
  • manage details of the apprentices they're currently training

Before you start

You need to have an account with the government’s Information Management Services system (IdAMS) to use this.

If your organisation doesn't already have an IdAMS account, you’ll get your account details when you register as an apprenticeship training provider.

If your organisation does already have an IdAMS account, an account super user from your organisation will need to give you access to the apprenticeship service.
