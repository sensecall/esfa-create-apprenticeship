Reserve funds and stuff

MVP

MVP assumptions
  • “Reserve funding” service will not be citizen facing to begin with
  • Employer already has an AS account
  • Funds can only be reserved for a “legal entity” (so the entity may need to be confirmed this with the user at some point in the journey)
  • We will focus on the logged in, account-facing journey first
  • Employer might have already signed their agreement, or might sign it after checking that funding is available
  • Employer is aware that they need to reserve funding (somehow)
  • There will only be rules/restrictions on start date, course level and the number of reservations
  • Employer may not know what the next steps are after they have reserved funds

A pared-down concept that contains the smallest number of steps.
